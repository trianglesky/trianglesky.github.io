A friend bought me some lino printing equipment for Christmas. It's fun and I completely recommend it.

List of stuff used to make these images:

- Piece of lino
- Lino cutting tool
- Seawhites block printing ink
- Ink roller
- Piece of acrylic to hold the ink
- A3 paper
