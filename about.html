about


I’m in my early thirties and live in Edinburgh.

I started drawing comics and mandalas in 2003.

Between 2004 and 2006, I self-published three comics: Sizemo and Satchmo versus Humanity; The Passionate Intensity of Neil Armstrong; and Tales from the Cosmic Mountain.

Between 2006 and 2011, I drew a webcomic called Carl Garbanzo.

More recently, I’ve contributed to a few issues of the Comix Reader, a newspaper comic edited by Richard Cowdry.
